# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-26

GymCalc does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data.

## Data Collection

- We do not collect personal information.
- We do not collect analytics data.
- We do not collect location data.
- We do not collect device identifiers for tracking.

## Local Device Storage

GymCalc stores app settings locally on your device (for example, calculator preferences) so the app can remember your choices. This local data is not sent to any server.

## Third-Party Services

GymCalc does not use third-party services for advertising, analytics, or backend data collection.

## Children

GymCalc does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in this file with a new effective date.
